# 文法圖生成風格指南 (Grammar Image Generation Styles)

這份文件記錄了用於生成英文文法教學圖的各種 AI 繪圖詠唱（Prompt）風格範本。未來若有新的風格，將持續新增於此。

---

## 風格一：魔法童話漫畫風 (Pastel Crayon Magical Comic)

此風格專注於用柔和的粉蠟筆色調與魔法童話元素，結合漫畫分鏡（Comic book layout）來呈現文法情境。特色是必須包含具體的英文對話框，以及一個提示常見錯誤的魔法警告牌。

### 核心元素 (Core Elements)
*   **視覺風格 (Visual Style)**: Pastel crayon style, magical storybook theme. (粉蠟筆風格，魔法童話主題)
*   **版面配置 (Layout)**: The art should have a comic book layout. (漫畫分鏡排版)
*   **角色與情境 (Characters & Scene)**: 依據文法主題與例句設定情境（例如：正在施法的巫師、看著水晶球等）。
*   **對話框內容 (Dialogue Bubbles)**: 明確要求對話框內包含指定的英文句子，使用 `MUST CLEARLY CONTAIN English text: "..."`。
*   **錯誤提示 (Warning Sign)**: 畫面中需有一個魔法警告標誌，用來提示學生常犯的文法錯誤，格式為 `A magical warning sign shows: "Caution: '...' is WRONG!"`。

### 🤖 AI 詠唱公式 (Prompt Formula)

在生成圖片時，請替換括號 `[...]` 內的變數：

```text
An educational comic poster for English [文法主題/細項]. Pastel crayon style, magical storybook theme. [角色與具體情境描述]. Dialogue speech bubbles MUST CLEARLY CONTAIN English text: "[例句1]", "[例句2]". A magical warning sign shows: "Caution: '[常犯的錯誤用法]' is WRONG!". The art should have a comic book layout.
```

### 💡 實際範例 (Examples)

**範例 1：未來簡單式 (will - 肯定/否定/疑問)**
> An educational comic poster for English Future Simple 'will' (Affirmative, Negative, Yes/No Questions). Pastel crayon style, magical storybook theme. A young wizard looking into a crystal ball or helping a friend. Dialogue speech bubbles MUST CLEARLY CONTAIN English text: "I'll help you.", "It won't rain.", and "Will they win?". A magical warning sign shows: "Caution: 'will to go' or 'willn't' is WRONG!". The art should have a comic book layout.

**範例 2：未來簡單式 (be going to - 肯定/否定/疑問)**
> An educational comic poster for English Future Simple 'be going to' (Affirmative, Negative, Yes/No Questions). Pastel crayon style, magical storybook theme. A wizard packing a bag for a trip. Dialogue speech bubbles MUST CLEARLY CONTAIN English text: "I am going to study.", "We aren't going to buy a car.", and "Are you going to stay here?". A magical warning sign shows: "Caution: 'I going to play' is WRONG! Don't forget 'am/is/are'!". The art should have a comic book layout.
