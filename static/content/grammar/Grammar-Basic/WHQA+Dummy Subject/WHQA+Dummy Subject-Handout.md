# Wh- 疑問句 (Asking Questions)
### **學好 Wh- 疑問句，最重要的是掌握兩件事：________________ 和 ________________。**

---

## A. 疑問句倒裝與虛主詞

### 1. 疑問句句型 — 倒裝
排問句時，核心是**倒裝**：把_______________&______________移到________________。

*   **公式一：句中有 Be 動詞 (am / is / are / was / were)**
    
    👉 **________________+ ________ + _______ …?**
    *   ________ are you? (你在哪裡？)
    *   What ________ she doing? (她在做什麼？)

*   **公式二：句中是一般動詞 (如 go, eat, live)**
    
    👉 **______________ + ____________________+ _______ + ________ …?**
    *   Where ________ he live? (他住在哪裡？)
    *   What ________ you ________? (你買了什麼？)
### 2. 虛主詞 It

**英文規則：________________________________。**

談論**時間、距離、天氣、星期、日期**等沒有具體「人／物」在做事時，常用 **_______________________** 佔位子；此時 **It ________________**。

*   **什麼時候常用虛主詞 It？**
    *   **___________**：What time is it? 👉 **________** is 7:00.
    *   **___________**：How far is it? 👉 **________** is three miles.
    *   **___________**：How long does it take? 👉 **________** takes 20 minutes.
    *   **___________**：How is the weather? 👉 **________** is raining.
    *   **___________**：What day is it today? 👉 **________** is Monday.

*   **代名詞 It（與虛主詞不同）**  
    回答**________________**時，用 **_____** 代替名詞，避免重複：
    *   Where is the cat? 👉 **________** is under the desk. (牠在書桌下。)

---
## B.疑問詞選擇
### **1. When (何時) / What time (幾點) &時間介係詞 (in / on / at)**

*   **When 與 What time 的差異**
    *   **________**：範圍大 — 可回答**幾點、星期幾、日期、月份、年份**等。
    *   **________**：範圍小 — 專問**幾點幾分**（具體時刻）。

*   #### 問句

    【Be 動詞】
    *   ________ is the meeting? (會議是何時？)
    *   ________ is the movie? (電影是幾點？)

    【一般動詞】
    *   ________________ you wake up? (你何時起床？)
    *   ________________ you wake up? (你幾點起床？)

*   #### 答句 
    【時間介係詞 (in / on / at)】
    *   **________ + 明確時刻或固定時段用語**：
    
         ________________ (在7:00)、 ________________ (在正午)、 ________________ (在午夜)、 ________________ (在晚上)
    *   **________ + 特定「日子」**：
    
         ________________ (在星期一)、 ________________ (在5月1日)、 ________________ (在我的生日)、 ________________ (在週末)
    *   **________ + 較長時段**：
    
         ________________ (在2026年)、 ________________ (在夏天)、 ________________ (在早上)、 ________________ (在下午/晚上)

     📌**零介係詞陷阱**  
    時間前有 **______________________** 時，**不要再加** in / on / at：
    *   ❌ on tomorrow → ⭕ ________
    *   ❌ in last year → ⭕ ________
*   **【詳答例句】**
    *   *問* ：What time do you wake up? → *答* ：：________ 7:00. / I wake up ________ 7:00.
    *   *問* ：When is your birthday? → *答*： ________ May 12th. / It's ________ May 12th.
    *   *問* ：When do you study English? → *答* ：________ the evening. / I study ________ the evening.


---

### **2. Where (哪裡) 與地方介係詞**
*   #### 問句
    【Be 動詞】
    *   Where ________ you? (你在哪裡？)
    *   ________________________ (貓在哪裡？)

    【一般動詞】
    *   Where ________ he live? (他住在哪裡？)
    *   ________________________ (你昨天去哪裡？)
*   #### 答句

    **【單字即地點（前面不加介係詞）答句】**
    *   I'm ________. (我在這裡。)
    *   She is ________. (她在那裡。)
    *   He is ________. (他在樓上。)
    *   They are ________. (他們在市區。)
    *   *問* Where is Maria? → *答* She is ________. (她在裡面。) / She is ________. (她在外面。)

    **【地方介係詞（介係詞 + 名詞）】**
    *   **in**（在…裡面）：________________________________ (在他的房間裡)、_________________________ (在箱子裡)
    *   **on**（在…上面，接觸表面）：___________ (在桌上)、_____________ (在公車上)
    *   **under**（在…正下方）：________________ (在書桌下)
    *   **at**（明確地點或狀態）：**________________** (在圖書館)、**________________** (在上班)、**____________________** (在公車站)
    *   **above**（上方，不接觸）：________________ (在箱子上方)
    *   **next to / beside**（旁邊）：________________ (在Maria旁邊)
    *   **behind**（後面）：________________ (在門後)
    *   **between**（兩者之間）：________________ ________________ (在銀行和公園之間)
    *   **in front of**（前面）：________________ (在電視前面)
    *   **across from / opposite**（對面）：________ ________ (在醫院對面)
    *   *例句*：The cat is ________ the desk. / Bob is ________ the library.

---
### **3. What (什麼)、What + 名詞、Which (哪一個)** 

#### * **What（事物／動作）**
【Be 動詞】
    
________ is that? → It is a book.

【一般動詞】 

________ do you want? → I want an apple.

#### * **What + 名詞（問得更細）**
【Be 動詞】

 ________ is it? / ________ is your car?

【一般動詞】

________ shoes did you buy? → Running shoes.

#### * **Which（在有限選項中選一個）**
【Be 動詞】
    
________ is your car, the red one or the blue one?

【一般動詞】

 ________ pen do you want? → I want the red one.

 可接單／複數名詞：________ earrings are yours? 
 
 也可問人：________ one is your daughter?

### * **Which vs. What 的差異**
**________**：選項**有限**（桌上兩支筆選一支）。

**________**：**開放式**（生日禮金想買什麼都可以說）。

---

### **4. What … do（各種時態的動作）& What do you do?**
### * **What + a form of do？ 做什麼／活動**
*   現在習慣：What ________ Bob ________ every morning? → He goes to class.
*   過去：What ________ you ________ yesterday? → I went downtown.
*   現在進行：What ________ Anna ________? → She is studying.
*   未來：What ________ you ________ tomorrow? → I'm going to the beach.
*   想要：What do you ________ tonight? / What would you ________ tomorrow?

### * **What do you do? 你做什麼職業?**  
不是問「你現在在做什麼」，而是問**職業**（≈ What do you do for a living?）：

*答* ：I'm a ________.(我是一位學生) / I am a ________.(我是一位學生)

---

### **5. How (如何) - 問「狀態」和「交通方式」**
#### * **搭配 Be 動詞，問「狀態」**
* ________ are you? → I'm ________, thanks.
* ________ is your brother?（你哥哥還好嗎？) → _____________________ （他很棒。) 
* ________ was the trip?（旅程還好嗎？) → 
    _____________________ （這個旅程很糟糕。) 

#### * **搭配一般動詞 — 問「交通方式」**
* 問句
  * ________ do you go to school?
  * ______________________________ ?（她是如何去上班？)
    
* 答句
  * On（可站立、行走、大空間）: ____________ (搭公車) , __________ (搭火車) , _____________ (搭飛機)
    
  * In（需縮身、小空間）:  ____________ (搭汽車) , __________ (搭計程車) , _____________ (搭卡車)
    
  * By（交通方式，前面不用加冠詞）:  ____________ (搭計程車) , __________ (搭公車) , _____________ (搭飛機)

### **6. How often (多常) & How long (多久)**

#### **How often（多常／頻率）**

* 問句

   * ________ are you at the library?

   * ________ was he sick last month?

   * ________ do you play tennis?

* 答句

  * **頻率副詞**（Be 後、一般動詞前）：**____________**、**____________**、**____________**、**____________**、**____________**、**____________**

    *例*： I ________ play tennis.
        
  * **次數片語**（常放句尾）：**____________**（一個月一次）、**____________**（一個年兩次）、**____________**（每天）

    *例*：________. （一個年兩次）/  I play tennis ________. （我一個月打一次網球。）/ He was sick ________. （他之前每天生病。）

#### **How long（多久／時間長度）**
* 問句

   【Be 動詞】
   
   ________ is the movie? / ________ will you be in Hong Kong?

   【一般動詞】
   
   ________ did you study last night? / ________ does it take to cook rice?

* 答句

  * **花費時間黃金公式：It ________ + (某人) + 時間 + ________ + ________.** 

    *例*：It ________ 20 minutes ________ rice. / It ________ Al two hours ________ to work.
   * 直接回答：It's ________./ ________. / ________.

### **7. 其他 How 的用法 — How far / How old / How much / How many**

#### **How far（多遠／距離）**
* 問句

   【Be 動詞】
   
   ________ is it from Mumbai to Delhi?

  【一般動詞】

  ________ do you live from school?

  也可用單位發問：________ is it …?（多少公里...?）/ ________ …?（多少公尺...?）
* 答句
    
    It is + 距離 + ________ A ________ B.（**from … to …** 與 **to … from …** 可互換，意思相同）  
        *例*： It is 3,605 miles ________ Moscow ________ Beijing./ ________________________./ ________________________________.

#### **How old（幾歲）**
________ are you? → I'm ________./ She is ten years old.

#### **How much（________ /________的數量）**
*   價錢：________ is this? → A little.
*   不可數：________ water do you need? → It's 100 dollars.

#### **How many（________的數量）** 
________ apples do you want? → Three, please.

### **8. Who (誰)**

  【Be動詞】

   ________ is that boy? → He is my brother. / That's John.

  【一般動詞】

  ________ do you like? →  I like my teacher.
  
  ________ did you call? → I called my mom.


📌 Who 直接當「主詞」問「誰做了這件事」，**不要**再加 do / does / did，動詞直接跟在後面：

⭕ Who ________ the window? / Who ________ some ice cream?

❌ Who did break the window?（不要這樣說）

---

### **9. Why（原因 )**

  【Be動詞】

________ are you late? →  ________ the bus was late. / Because I overslept.
  
  【一般動詞】

________ do you cry? →  ________ I am sad.
 
📌 答句常用 **Because** 開頭；口語也可用 **Because of + 名詞**：________ the rain.

---

### **10. How about / What about（提議與反問）+ 介係詞留在句尾**

#### * **How about / What about（意思相同，口語常用，較少用在正式寫作）**
*   **接名詞**：________ three o'clock?
*   **接 V-ing**：________ ________ to the zoo? / ________ ________ Sally over for dinner?

#### * **介係詞留在句尾（動詞片語習慣如此）
*   Where do you come ________?
*   ________________________________? （你在找甚麼？)

---

### **11. Be 動詞與助動詞的 Tag Questions（附加問句）**

**附加問句加在直述句________**，前面肯定 → ________；前面否定 → ________。

  【Be動詞】

Jill is sick, ________?

Marie isn't from Athens, ________?

There's enough time, ________?

I'm not late, ________? / I'm late, ________?（**I'm …** 的附加問句常用 **aren't I**）

  【一般動詞】

You know Bill, ________?

You don't know Tom, ________?

📌**語調表示的涵義**

 * **上揚 ⤴**：較不確定，想**確認**對方是否同意。
 
 * **下降 ⤵**：較確定，想**尋求認同**。

## 14. 考前檢查表：你中了幾個常犯錯誤？

寫完疑問句後，可用下面各單元易錯點自我檢查：

1. **有沒有漏掉助動詞或 Be 動詞？**  
   ❌ Where you live? → ⭕ Where ________ you live?
   
   ❌ Where the cat? → ⭕ Where ________ the cat?
   
   ❌ Why you late? → ⭕ Why ________ you late?

2. **順序對了嗎？（Be／助動詞有沒有移到主詞前）**  
   ❌ What you are doing? → ⭕ What ________ you doing?

3. **一般動詞有沒有還原成原形？**（did / does / do 後面要原形）  
   ❌ Where did you went? → ⭕ Where did you ________?  
   ❌ What does he wants? → ⭕ What does he ________?

4. **有沒有誤用 Be 動詞取代助動詞？**（live 等一般動詞要用 do/does）  
   ❌ Where are you live? → ⭕ Where ________ you live?

5. **有沒有誤加多餘的介係詞、助動詞或冠詞？**

   ❌ on tomorrow / in last year → ⭕ ________ / ________

   ❌ at here → ⭕ ________

   ❌ Who did break the window? → ⭕ Who ________ the window?

   ❌ by a bus → ⭕ ________ bus / ________ a bus

   ❌ between A to B → ⭕ between A ________ B

6. **有沒有搞錯疑問詞？**

   ❌ Which color is your car? → ⭕ ________ color is your car? 

   ❌ How are you? → *答* By bus. (狀態 vs 交通方式混淆)

   ❌ How long is it from Mumbai to Delhi? → ⭕ ________ is it from Mumbai to Delhi? 

   ❌ How many water? → ⭕ ________ water? 

7. **有沒有搞錯介係詞或固定搭配？**

   ❌ in Monday → ⭕ ________ Monday
   ❌ by foot → ⭕ ________ foot
   ❌ What are you looking? → ⭕ What are you looking ________?

8. **虛主詞與答句主詞是否正確？**

   ❌ *問天氣/時間/距離* → *答* He is 7:00. → ⭕ ________ is 7:00.

   ❌ How long does it take? → *答* It is 20 minutes. → ⭕ It ________ 20 minutes.

9. **附加問句 (Tag Questions) 的規則對了嗎？**

   ❌ Jill is sick, is she? → ⭕ Jill is sick, ________?

   ❌ I'm late, amn't I? → ⭕ I'm late, ________?

   ❌ There's enough time, isn't it? → ⭕ There's enough time, ________?
   
   ❌ You like it, isn't you? → ⭕ You like it, ________?

# 【解答版】文法講義：Wh- 疑問句 (Asking Questions) — WHQA

*(教師小撇步：標記 ==高亮== 的文字為學生版的填空處，可透過尋找取代 ==.*?== 為 ________ 一鍵轉換為學生考卷！)*

**學好 Wh- 疑問句，最重要的是掌握兩件事：==排對字詞的順序（倒裝句型）== 和 ==選對疑問詞==。**

---

## 1. 疑問句句型 — 倒裝公式

### 疑問句句型 — 倒裝
排問句時，核心是**倒裝**：把 ==Be 動詞== & ==助動詞== 移到 ==主詞前面==。

*   **公式一：句中有 Be 動詞 (am / is / are / was / were)**
    
    👉 **==疑問詞==+ ==Be 動詞== + ==主詞== …?**
    *   ==Where== are you? (你在哪裡？)
    *   What ==is== she doing? (她在做什麼？)

*   **公式二：句中是一般動詞 (如 go, eat, live)**
    
    👉 **==疑問詞== + ==助動詞 (do/does/did)==+ ==主詞== + ==原形動詞== …?**
    *   Where ==does== he live? (他住在哪裡？)
    *   What ==did== you ==buy==? (你買了什麼？)
### 虛主詞 It

**英文規則：==每個句子都要有主詞==。**

談論**時間、距離、天氣、星期、日期**等沒有具體「人／物」在做事時，常用 **==虛主詞 (Dummy Subject) It==** 佔位子；此時 **It ==不必翻成「它」==**。

*   **什麼時候常用虛主詞 It？**
    *   **==時間==**：What time is it? 👉 **==It==** is 7:00.
    *   **==距離==**：How far is it? 👉 **==It==** is three miles.
    *   **==花費時間==**：How long does it take? 👉 **==It==** takes 20 minutes.
    *   **==天氣==**：How is the weather? 👉 **==It==** is raining.
    *   **==星期／日期==**：What day is it today? 👉 **==It==** is Monday.

*   **代名詞 It（與虛主詞不同）**  
    回答**==已提過的單一物品或動物==**時，用 **==It==** 代替名詞，避免重複：
    *   Where is the cat? 👉 **==It==** is under the desk. (牠在書桌下。)

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- 漏 **do/does/did** 或 **Be**（*Where you live?*、*What he wants?*）。
- **倒裝順序錯**（*Where you are?*、*What you are doing?*）。
- **助動詞後動詞未還原**（*Did you went?*、*Does he likes?*）。
- 一般動詞句誤用 **Be**（*Where are you live?*）— 應 **Where do you live?**
- 時間／天氣／距離答句主詞錯（*He is 7:00.*、*The weather is rain.*）。
- **How long does it take** 答成 *It is 20 minutes.* — 應 **It takes …**。
- **What time is?** 漏 **it** — 應 **What time is it?**

## 3. When (何時) / What time (幾點) &時間介係詞 (in / on / at)

*   **When 與 What time 的差異**
    *   **==When==**：範圍大 — 可回答**幾點、星期幾、日期、月份、年份**等。
    *   **==What time==**：範圍小 — 專問**幾點幾分**（具體時刻）。

*   ### 問句

    **【Be 動詞】**
    *   ==When== is the meeting? (會議是何時？)
    *   ==What time== is the movie? (電影是幾點？)

    **【一般動詞】**
    *   ==When do== you wake up? (你何時起床？)
    *   ==What time do== you wake up? (你幾點起床？)

*   ### 答句 
    **【時間介係詞 (in / on / at)】**
    *   **==at== + 明確時刻或固定時段用語**：
    
         ==at 7:00== (在7:00)、 ==at noon== (在正午)、 ==at midnight== (在午夜)、 ==at night== (在晚上)
    *   **==on== + 特定「日子」**：
    
         ==on Monday== (在星期一)、 ==on May 1st== (在5月1日)、 ==on my birthday== (在我的生日)、 ==on weekends== (在週末)
    *   **==in== + 較長時段**：
    
         ==in 2026== (在2026年)、 ==in summer== (在夏天)、 ==in the morning== (在早上)、 ==in the afternoon/evening== (在下午/晚上)

     📌**零介係詞陷阱**  
    時間前有 **==this/that/next/last/every/today/tomorrow/yesterday==** 時，**不要再加** in / on / at：
    *   ❌ on tomorrow → ⭕ ==tomorrow==
    *   ❌ in last year → ⭕ ==last year==
*   **【詳答例句】**
    *   *問* What time do you wake up? → *答* ==At== 7:00. / I wake up ==at== 7:00.
    *   *問* When is your birthday? → *答* ==On== May 12th. / It's ==on== May 12th.
    *   *問* When do you study English? → *答* ==In== the evening. / I study ==in== the evening.


---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- 選擇題：問「精確幾點」卻只選 **When**（題意常要求 **What time**）。
- **When is you wake up?**／**When do you the meeting?** — **Be** 與 **do** 句型混用。
- 無一般動詞卻寫 *When does the movie?* — 應 **When is …?** / **What time is …?**
- **in/on/at** 選錯（*in Monday*、*on 7:00*、*at July*）。
- **next/last/this/every/today/tomorrow** 前又多 **on/in/at**（*on tomorrow*）。

## 4. Where (哪裡) 與地方介係詞
*   ### 問句
    **【Be 動詞】**
    *   Where ==are== you? (你在哪裡？)
    *   ==Where is the cat?== (貓在哪裡？)

    **【一般動詞】**
    *   Where ==does== he live? (他住在哪裡？)
    *   ==Where did you go yesterday?== (你昨天去哪裡？)
*   ### 答句

*   **【單字即地點（前面不加介係詞）答句】**
    *   I'm ==here==. (我在這裡。)
    *   She is ==there==. (她在那裡。)
    *   He is ==upstairs==. (他在樓上。)
    *   They are ==downtown==. (他們在市區。)
    *   *問* Where is Maria? → *答* She is ==inside==. (她在裡面。) / She is ==outside==. (她在外面。)

*   **【地方介係詞（介係詞 + 名詞）】**
    *   **in**（在…裡面）：==in his room== (在他的房間裡)、==in the box== (在箱子裡)
    *   **on**（在…上面，接觸表面）：==on the table== (在桌上)、==on the bus== (在公車上)
    *   **under**（在…正下方）：==under the desk== (在書桌下)
    *   **at**（明確地點或狀態）：==at the library== (在圖書館)、==at work== (在上班)、==at the bus stop== (在公車站)
    *   **above**（上方，不接觸）：==above the box== (在箱子上方)
    *   **next to / beside**（旁邊）：==next to Maria== (在Maria旁邊)
    *   **behind**（後面）：==behind the door== (在門後)
    *   **between**（兩者之間）：==between the bank== and ==the park== (在銀行和公園之間)
    *   **in front of**（前面）：==in front of the TV== (在電視前面)
    *   **across from / opposite**（對面）：==across from== ==the hospital== (在醫院對面)
    *   *例句*：The cat is ==under== the desk. / Bob is ==at== the library.

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- ***Where are you live?*** — 應 **Where do you live?** 或 **Where are you?**
- **here/there/upstairs** 前多加 **at/in**（*at here*）。
- **Where** 問句漏 **Be** 或 **do**（*Where the cat?*）。
- **on the bus** 與 **in the car**／**in a taxi** 慣用搭配寫反。
- **next to** 漏 **to**（*next the chair*）。
- **between A to B** ❌ — 應 **between A and B**。

## 5. What (什麼)、What + 名詞、Which (哪一個) 

### What（事物／動作）
**【Be 動詞】** 

==What== is that? → It is a book.

**【一般動詞】** 

==What== do you want? → I want an apple.

### What + 名詞（問得更細）
**【Be 動詞】** 

 ==What time== is it? / ==What color== is your car?

**【一般動詞】** 

==What kind of== shoes did you buy? → Running shoes.

### Which（在有限選項中選一個）
**【Be 動詞】** 
    
==Which== is your car, the red one or the blue one?

**【一般動詞】**

 ==Which== pen do you want? → I want the red one.

 可接單／複數名詞：==Which== earrings are yours? 
 
 也可問人：==Which== one is your daughter?

### Which vs. What 的差異
**==Which==**：選項**有限**（桌上兩支筆選一支）。

**==What==**：**開放式**（生日禮金想買什麼都可以說）。

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- 二選一情境仍只用 **What**（考題常要求 **Which**）。
- ***Which color …?*** ❌ — 顏色用 **What color …?**
- **What**／**Which** 與後接名詞、冠詞搭配錯（依題幹檢查）。

## 6. What … do（各種時態的動作）& What do you do?
### What + a form of do？ 做什麼／活動
*   現在習慣：What ==does== Bob ==do== every morning? → He goes to class.
*   過去：What ==did== you ==do== yesterday? → I went downtown.
*   現在進行：What ==is== Anna ==doing==? → She is studying.
*   未來：What ==are== you ==going to do== tomorrow? → I'm going to the beach.
*   想要：What do you ==want to do== tonight? / What would you ==like to do== tomorrow?

### What do you do? 你做什麼職業?**  
不是問「你現在在做什麼」，而是問**職業**（≈ What do you do for a living?）：

*答* ：I'm a ==student==.(我是一位學生) / I am a ==student==.(我是一位學生)

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- **What do you do?** 答「正在做的事」— 題意是問**職業**。
- **did** 後動詞未原形（*What did you went?*）。
- **What are you doing?** 與 **What do you do?** 未分清楚就套錯。

## 7. How (如何) 
### 搭配 Be 動詞，問「狀態」
* ==How== are you? → I'm ==fine==, thanks.
* ==How== is your brother?（你哥哥還好嗎？) → ==He is great.== （他很棒。) 
* ==How== was the trip?（旅程還好嗎？) → 
    ==It was terrible.== （這個旅程很糟糕。) 

### 搭配一般動詞 — 問「交通方式」
* **問句**
  * ==How== do you go to school?
  * ==How does she get to work== ?（她是如何去上班？)
    
* **答句**
  * On（可站立、行走、大空間）: ==on the bus== (搭公車) , ==on the train== (搭火車) , ==on the plane== (搭飛機)
    
  * In（需縮身、小空間）:  ==in the car== (搭汽車) , ==in the taxi== (搭計程車) , ==in the truck== (搭卡車)
    
  * By（交通方式，前面不用加冠詞）:  ==by taxi== (搭計程車) , ==by bus== (搭公車) , ==by air== (搭飛機)

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- **How are you?** 答成 *By bus.* — 應答**狀態／感受**。
- **How** 與 **What** 問近况／身分混淆。
- **by bus** 中間加 **a/the**（*by a bus*）。
- 走路寫成 *by foot* — 應 **on foot**。
- **How** 與 **Where** 問「方式／地點」選錯。

## 8. How often (多常) & How long (多久)

### How often（多常／頻率）

* **問句**

   * ==How often== are you at the library?

   * ==How often== was he sick last month?

   * ==How often== do you play tennis?

* **答句**

  * **頻率副詞**（Be 後、一般動詞前）：**==always==**、**==usually==**、**==often==**、**==sometimes==**、**==seldom==**、**==never==**

    *例*： I ==usually== play tennis.
        
  * **次數片語**（常放句尾）：**==once a month==**（一個月一次）、**==twice a year==**（一個年兩次）、**==every day==**（每天）

    *例*：==Twice a year==. （一個年兩次）/  I play tennis ==once a month==. （我一個月打一次網球。）/ He was sick ==every day==. （他之前每天生病。）

### How long（多久／時間長度）
* **問句**

   **【Be 動詞】** 
   
   ==How long== is the movie? / ==How long== will you be in Hong Kong?

   **【一般動詞】**
   
   ==How long== did you study last night? / ==How long== does it take to cook rice?

* **答句**

  * **花費時間黃金公式：It ________ + (某人) + 時間 + ________ + ________.** 

    *例*：It ==takes== 20 minutes ==to cook== rice. / It ==took== Al two hours ==to drive== to work.
   * 直接回答：It's ==two hours==./ ==Ten days==. / ==Four hours==.

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- 頻率副詞位置錯（*I play usually tennis.*）— **usually** 在一般動詞前；**Be** 後則 *He is usually …*。
- **How often** 與 **How long** 選錯。
- 花費時間答成 *It is 20 minutes to …* — 應 **It takes …**。
- **How long** 與 **How far** 混淆。
- **did** 後用過去式（*How long did you stayed?*）。

## 9. 其他 How 的用法 — How far / How old / How much / How many

### How far（多遠／距離）
* **問句**

   **【Be 動詞】** 
   
   ==How far== is it from Mumbai to Delhi?

  **【一般動詞】**

  ==How far== do you live from school?

  也可用單位發問：==How many kilometers== is it …?（多少公里...?）/ ==How many meters== …?（多少公尺...?）
* **答句**
    
    It is + 距離 + ==from== A ==to== B.（**from … to …** 與 **to … from …** 可互換，意思相同）  
        *例*： It is 3,605 miles ==from== Moscow ==to== Beijing./ ==It's 725 miles==./ ==I live four blocks from school==.

### How old（幾歲）
==How old== are you? → I'm ==twelve==./ She is ten years old.

### How much（==價錢== /==不可數==的量）
*   價錢：==How much== is this? → It's 100 dollars.
*   不可數：==How much== water do you need? → A little.

### How many（==可數==的數量） 
==How many== apples do you want? → Three, please.

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- **How far** 與 **How long** 選錯。
- 答距離 **from/to** 漏寫或 **It is** 結構錯。
- **How many** 後接單數或不可數（*How many water*）— 可數用 **How many**，不可數用 **How much**。
- **How much** 與 **How many** 互選錯。
- **How old** 答成 *I have twelve years old.* — 應 **I'm twelve.** / **I'm twelve years old.**

## 10. Who (誰) 

  **【Be動詞】**

   ==Who== is that boy? → He is my brother. / That's John.

  **【一般動詞】**

  ==Who== do you like? →  I like my teacher.
  
  ==Who== did you call? → I called my mom.


📌 Who 直接當「主詞」問「誰做了這件事」，**不要**再加 do / does / did，動詞直接跟在後面：

⭕ Who ==broke== the window? / Who ==wants== some ice cream?

❌ Who did break the window?（不要這樣說）

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- **Who is** 與主詞單複數不一致（*Who are that boy?*）。
- **Who** 問句漏 **do/does** 或 **did** 後未原形。
- **Who** 當主詞卻加 **did/does/do**（*Who did break…?*）。
- **Who want** vs **Who wants** 單複數一致問題。

## 11. Why（原因 )

  **【Be動詞】**

==Why== are you late? →  ==Because== the bus was late. / Because I overslept.
  
  **【一般動詞】**

==Why== do you cry? →  ==Because== I am sad.
 
📌 答句常用 **Because** 開頭；口語也可用 **Because of + 名詞**：==Because of== the rain.

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- *Why you late?* — 應 **Why are you late?**／**Why do you …?**
- **Because** 後接不完整（*Because tired.*）— 應子句或改 **Because of …**。

## 12. How about / What about（提議與反問）+ 介係詞留在句尾

### How about / What about（意思相同，口語常用，較少用在正式寫作）
*   **接名詞**：==How about== three o'clock?
*   **接 V-ing**：==How about== ==going== to the zoo? / ==What about== ==asking== Sally over for dinner?

### 介係詞留在句尾（動詞片語習慣如此）
*   Where do you come ==from==?
*   ==What are you looking for==? （你在找甚麼？)

---


**📌 考試最常錯**
- **How about go …** — 應 **How about going …?**
- **What are you looking?** 漏 **for**。
- 介係詞與動詞搭配拆錯（*Where do you from come?*）。

## 13. Be 動詞與助動詞的 Tag Questions（附加問句）

**【問句】附加問句加在直述句後面**，前面肯定 → 後面否定；前面否定 → 後面肯定。

  **【Be動詞】**

Jill is sick, ==isn't she==?

Marie isn't from Athens, ==is she==?

There's enough time, ==isn't there==?

I'm not late, ==am I==? / I'm late, ==aren't I==?（**I'm …** 的附加問句常用 **aren't I**）

  **【一般動詞】**

You know Bill, ==don't you==?

You don't know Tom, ==do you==?

📌**語調表示的涵義**

 * **上揚 ⤴**：較不確定，想**確認**對方是否同意。
 
 * **下降 ⤵**：較確定，想**尋求認同**。

---

**📌 考試最常錯**
- **前肯後否／前否後肯**搞反。
- **I'm …, aren't I?**／**I'm not …, am I?** 寫錯（*amn't I?*）。
- **There's …, isn't there?** 寫成 *isn't it?*。
- 一般動詞附加問句用錯（*You like it, isn't you?*）— 應 **don't you?**

## 14. 考前檢查表：你中了幾個常犯錯誤？

寫完疑問句後，可用下面各單元易錯點自我檢查：

1. **有沒有漏掉助動詞或 Be 動詞？**  
   ❌ Where you live? → ⭕ Where ==do== you live?
   ❌ Where the cat? → ⭕ Where ==is== the cat?
   ❌ Why you late? → ⭕ Why ==are== you late?

2. **順序對了嗎？（Be／助動詞有沒有移到主詞前）**  
   ❌ What you are doing? → ⭕ What ==are== you doing?

3. **一般動詞有沒有還原成原形？**（did / does / do 後面要原形）  
   ❌ Where did you went? → ⭕ Where did you ==go==?  
   ❌ What does he wants? → ⭕ What does he ==want==?

4. **有沒有誤用 Be 動詞取代助動詞？**（live 等一般動詞要用 do/does）  
   ❌ Where are you live? → ⭕ Where ==do== you live?

5. **有沒有誤加多餘的介係詞、助動詞或冠詞？**
   ❌ on tomorrow / in last year → ⭕ ==tomorrow== / ==last year==
   ❌ at here → ⭕ ==here==
   ❌ Who did break the window? → ⭕ Who ==broke== the window?
   ❌ by a bus → ⭕ ==by bus== / ==on== a bus
   ❌ between A to B → ⭕ between A ==and== B

6. **有沒有搞錯疑問詞？**
   ❌ Which color is your car? → ⭕ ==What== color is your car? 
   ❌ How are you? → *答* By bus. (狀態 vs 交通方式混淆)
   ❌ How long is it from Mumbai to Delhi? → ⭕ ==How far== is it from Mumbai to Delhi? 
   ❌ How many water? → ⭕ ==How much== water? 

7. **有沒有搞錯介係詞或固定搭配？**
   ❌ in Monday → ⭕ ==on== Monday
   ❌ by foot → ⭕ ==on== foot
   ❌ What are you looking? → ⭕ What are you looking ==for==?

8. **虛主詞與答句主詞是否正確？**
   ❌ *問天氣/時間/距離* → *答* He is 7:00. → ⭕ ==It== is 7:00.
   ❌ How long does it take? → *答* It is 20 minutes. → ⭕ It ==takes== 20 minutes.

9. **附加問句 (Tag Questions) 的規則對了嗎？**
   ❌ Jill is sick, is she? → ⭕ Jill is sick, ==isn't she==?
   ❌ I'm late, amn't I? → ⭕ I'm late, ==aren't I==?
   ❌ There's enough time, isn't it? → ⭕ There's enough time, ==isn't there==?
   ❌ You like it, isn't you? → ⭕ You like it, ==don't you==?
